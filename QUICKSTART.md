# Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Register a new account**
   - Go to `/register`
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign up"

2. **Log in**
   - Go to `/login`
   - Enter your credentials
   - Click "Sign in"

3. **Create your first note**
   - Click the "New Note" button
   - Enter a title (optional)
   - Enter your note content
   - Click "Save" (or press Ctrl+Enter / Cmd+Enter)

4. **Manage your notes**
   - **Edit**: Click the edit icon on any note card
   - **Delete**: Click the trash icon
   - **Pin**: Click the pin icon to keep at top
   - **Search**: Use the search bar to filter notes
   - **Logout**: Click the logout button in the top right

## Features Explained

**Pinned Notes**: Important notes appear at the top of your list

**Search**: Searches through both titles and content in real-time

**Last Updated**: Each note shows when it was last modified

**Dark Theme**: Easy on the eyes for extended use

## Tips

- Press Ctrl+Enter (Cmd+Enter on Mac) to quickly save a note
- Search works instantly as you type
- Pin your most important notes for easy access
- All your notes are encrypted and only you can see them
